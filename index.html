Pig Game – Dice Game Web App

A classic two-player dice game implemented for the browser. Players take turns rolling a die to accumulate points in their current turn ("round score"). The catch: if a player rolls a 1, their round score resets to zero and it's the opponent’s turn. Players can choose to "hold" to save their accumulated round score to their global score. The first to reach the target score (usually 20 or 100) wins.
